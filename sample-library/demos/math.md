# Math Showcase

Inline math like $E = mc^2$, absolute values $\abs{x}$, and sets $\RR$, $\NN$, $\ZZ$
flow naturally with the surrounding text.

## Display equations

$$
\int_{-\infty}^{\infty} e^{-x^2}\,\dd x = \sqrt{\pi}
$$

The Cauchy–Schwarz inequality:

$$
\abs{\langle u, v\rangle} \le \norm{u}\,\norm{v}
$$

## Aligned & cases

$$
\begin{aligned}
f(x) &= (x+1)^2 \\
     &= x^2 + 2x + 1
\end{aligned}
$$

$$
\abs{x} =
\begin{cases}
x & \text{if } x \ge 0 \\
-x & \text{if } x < 0
\end{cases}
$$

## Matrices

$$
A = \begin{pmatrix} 1 & 2 \\ 3 & 4 \end{pmatrix}, \qquad \det A = -2
$$

## Chemistry (mhchem)

$$
\ce{CO2 + C -> 2 CO}
$$

$$
\ce{2 H2 + O2 -> 2 H2O}
$$

## A numbered identity

$$
e^{i\pi} + 1 = 0 \tag{Euler}
$$
