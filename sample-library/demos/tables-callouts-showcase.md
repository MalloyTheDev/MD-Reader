# Tables & Callouts Showcase

## Rich callout types

Callouts are written as `> [!type] Optional title`. Each type has its own color and icon.

> [!note] Note
> General information worth highlighting.

> [!tip] Tip
> A helpful suggestion or shortcut.

> [!warning] Heads up
> Something to be careful about.

> [!important] Important
> Don't miss this.

> [!hypothesis] Hypothesis
> Increasing the temperature will raise the reaction rate.

> [!method] Method
> Measure the rate at 10°C intervals from 20°C to 80°C.

> [!result] Result
> Rate roughly doubled every 10°C, consistent with the Arrhenius relationship.

> [!conclusion] Conclusion
> The data supports the hypothesis within experimental error.

> [!theorem] Pythagorean theorem
> For a right triangle, $a^2 + b^2 = c^2$.

> [!proof] Proof
> Follows from the law of cosines with the included angle equal to 90°. ∎

> [!quantum] Quantum note
> The wavefunction collapses on measurement.

> [!genetics] Genetics
> Recessive alleles are expressed only in homozygotes.

> [!experiment] Experiment
> Run the assay in triplicate and average the results.

> [!data] Data
> Raw values are stored alongside this note.

> [!todo] To do
>
> - [ ] Repeat with a control group

## Editor helpers

In the editor, use **CSV→Table** to paste spreadsheet data as a Markdown table, and **Table→CSV**
to copy a table back out as CSV.
